ROAS

Description

Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) measures the total revenue generated for every dollar spent on advertising. A higher ROAS indicates more effective and profitable ad campaigns.

Formula

ROAS = Revenue ÷ Ad Spend

Calculation

ROAS = $6,000.00 ÷ $1,000.00 = 6.0x

Description

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) measures the average cost of acquiring a single new customer through advertising. A lower CAC indicates more efficient customer acquisition.

Formula

CAC = Ad Spend ÷ New Customers

Calculation

CAC = $1,000.00 ÷ 50 = $20.00

Description

Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) estimates the total gross profit a business can expect to earn from a single customer over the entire duration of their relationship.

Formula

LTV = Monthly Revenue × Retention Months × Gross Margin

Calculation

LTV = $100.00 × 24 × 70% = $1,680.00

Description

The LTV to CAC ratio compares the lifetime value of a customer to the cost of acquiring that customer. A ratio above 3:1 is generally considered healthy for sustainable growth.

Formula

LTV : CAC = LTV ÷ CAC

Calculation

LTV:CAC = $1,680.00 ÷ $20.00 = 84.0x
